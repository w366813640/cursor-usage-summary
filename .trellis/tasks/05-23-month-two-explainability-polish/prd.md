# Month 2 Week 3: Explainability Polish

## Goal

Make recommendations easier to trust by surfacing assumptions, confidence, and source data near insights and scenarios.

## Requirements

- Add visible assumption/source copy to scenario cards.
- Keep language deterministic and tied to pure functions.
- Avoid speculative or AI-like phrasing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Scenario and action surfaces explain why they appear.
- Users can distinguish forecast, cache, top-burn, and volume assumptions.
- Typecheck and lint pass.
